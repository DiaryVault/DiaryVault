Overview

Our Privacy Commitment

DiaryVault is built on the principle that your personal thoughts and experiences belong to you. We are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how DiaryVault, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our digital journaling platform and marketplace services. By using DiaryVault, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted), and profile information
  • Payment Information: Billing details for marketplace transactions (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to our support team

Journal Content

  • Journal Entries: Your personal journal entries, including text, photos, and metadata
  • AI-Generated Content: Summaries, insights, and biographical content created by our AI
  • Preferences: Your journaling preferences, themes, and settings

Usage Information

  • Analytics Data: How you interact with our platform, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Cookies: Session data, preferences, and analytical information

Important Note

Your journal entries are private by default and are only made public if you explicitly choose to publish them on our marketplace.

How We Use Your Information

Core Services

  • Provide and maintain our journaling platform
  • Generate AI-powered insights and summaries
  • Create personalized biographical content
  • Enable marketplace functionality for published journals
  • Process payments and transactions

Communication

  • Send important account and service updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send optional marketing communications (with your consent)

Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Monitor and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure platform security and performance

AI Processing

We use your journal entries to generate personalized insights and summaries. This processing happens on secure servers and the AI models do not retain or learn from your specific content.

Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

DiaryVault does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your journal entries remain private unless you choose to publish them.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Published Content: Journal entries you choose to publish on our marketplace
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

Marketplace Sharing

When you publish journal entries on our marketplace:

  • The published content becomes publicly accessible
  • Your author profile information is shared with purchasers
  • Revenue and transaction data is shared for payment processing
  • Basic analytics may be shared to help you understand your audience

Data Security

Enterprise-Grade Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Server Security: Secure cloud infrastructure with regular updates and monitoring
  • Data Backups: Regular automated backups with redundancy
  • Incident Response: Comprehensive security incident response procedures

Your Role in Security

  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Keep your devices and browsers up to date
  • Log out from shared or public devices
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

Note: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security posture and will notify you of any significant security incidents affecting your data.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA Compliant)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

Account Controls

  • Update your profile and preferences in account settings
  • Control who can see your published journals
  • Manage email communication preferences
  • Delete individual journal entries or your entire account
  • Export your journal data at any time

Easy Data Management

Most data management tasks can be completed directly in your account settings. For complex requests, contact our support team and we'll respond within 30 days.

Account Deletion

If you choose to delete your account:

  • All private journal entries will be permanently deleted
  • Published marketplace content may remain accessible
  • Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes
  • Backups are purged within 90 days

Cookies and Tracking

Cookie Types

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality (login, security)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Support advertising and promotional features (optional)

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Stripe: Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Social Media: Login and sharing functionality

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings (block, delete, or manage cookies)
  • Our cookie preference center (when available)
  • Opt-out links for specific services

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Marketplace Privacy

Publishing Journals

When you publish content on our marketplace:

  • Published entries become publicly accessible
  • Your author profile is visible to users
  • Sales and revenue data is tracked for payments
  • User reviews and ratings are associated with your content

Buyer Privacy

  • Purchase history is kept private
  • Payment information is handled securely by Stripe
  • Reviews are optional and can be made anonymously
  • Contact between buyers and authors is facilitated through our platform

Revenue and Analytics

  • Authors receive detailed analytics about their content performance
  • Aggregate marketplace data may be used for platform improvements
  • Individual transaction details remain confidential
  • Tax reporting information is processed securely

Content Responsibility

Authors are responsible for ensuring they have rights to publish their content and that it complies with our community guidelines.

Children's Privacy

DiaryVault is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Teen Users (13-17)

  • Teens may use DiaryVault with parental consent
  • Parents can request access to their teen's account information
  • Additional protections apply to marketplace participation
  • Teens cannot publish content for sale without parental approval

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

International Users

DiaryVault operates globally and may transfer your data to countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Robust security measures regardless of location
  • Compliance with local privacy laws

Regional Compliance

  • GDPR (EU): Full compliance with European privacy regulations
  • CCPA (California): Consumer privacy rights protection
  • PIPEDA (Canada): Personal information protection compliance
  • Other Jurisdictions: Adherence to local privacy laws where applicable

Global Privacy Standards

We apply the highest privacy standards globally, ensuring consistent protection regardless of your location.
