Our Privacy Promise

🔐 "No email. No tracking. No ads. Just your words."

DiaryVault is built on the principle that your thoughts belong to you. We use cutting-edge encryption and Web3 technology to ensure your journal entries remain completely private unless you choose to share them.

DiaryVault is not like other platforms. We don't require email addresses. We don't track your browsing. We don't show ads. We don't sell your data. Instead, we've built a journaling platform that puts your privacy first and rewards you for writing.

What We Collect (And Why)

✨ The Magic: We Collect Very Little

Unlike traditional platforms, we intentionally collect minimal data. No email required. No profile building. No behavioral tracking.

👛 If You Connect a Wallet

  • Wallet Address: Your public wallet address (this is public by design)
  • Transaction History: Only transactions related to DiaryVault (tips, rewards, NFT mints)
  • Optional Username: A display name if you choose to set one

📝 Your Journal Content

  • Private Entries: Encrypted and stored securely - we literally cannot read them
  • Public Entries: Only if you explicitly choose to publish them
  • Entry Metadata: Date, word count, mood (if you choose to track it)

🔧 Essential Technical Data

  • Session Information: To keep you logged in (stored locally in your browser)
  • Error Logs: To fix bugs and improve the platform (anonymized)
  • Basic Analytics: How many people use certain features (no personal identifiers)

🔐 Anonymous by Default

You can use DiaryVault completely anonymously. No email, no phone number, no real name required. Just connect a wallet or use our anonymous mode.

Wallet & Web3 Privacy

We use Web3 technology to give you true ownership of your data. Here's how wallet connectivity works and what it means for your privacy:

🔑 Wallet Connection

  • Your Choice: Connecting a wallet is optional - you can write anonymously
  • No Personal Info: We never ask for your name, email, or other personal details
  • Public Address Only: We only see your wallet's public address (which is already public)
  • You Control Access: You can disconnect anytime and revoke all permissions

💰 Rewards & Transactions

  • On-Chain Rewards: Writing rewards are sent directly to your wallet
  • Transparent: All reward transactions are visible on the blockchain
  • No Middleman: We don't hold your tokens - they go straight to you
  • Withdrawal Anytime: Your rewards are yours immediately

🎨 NFTs & Collectibles

  • Optional Creation: You choose if/when to mint journal entries as NFTs
  • Private by Default: NFTs are only created when you explicitly request it
  • Your Ownership: NFTs belong to you, not us
  • Royalty Benefits: You earn from any future sales

🌐 Decentralized Storage

Your encrypted journal entries can be stored on decentralized networks like Arweave, ensuring they're preserved forever and can't be censored or deleted by any company (including us).

How We Encrypt Your Data

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Your journal entries are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers. This means we literally cannot read your entries, even if we wanted to.

🛡️ Encryption Process

  • Client-Side Encryption: Your entries are encrypted in your browser using your wallet's private key
  • Zero-Knowledge: We never see your unencrypted content
  • Secure Key Management: Encryption keys are derived from your wallet signature
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy: Each entry uses a unique encryption key

🔒 Technical Details

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM encryption with ChaCha20-Poly1305 fallback
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Transport Security: TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
  • Storage Security: Encrypted at rest with separate keys

🔑 Access Control

  • Only You Can Decrypt: Your wallet signature is required to decrypt entries
  • No Master Keys: We don't have backdoors or master decryption keys
  • Selective Sharing: You can grant decryption access to specific entries only
  • Revocable Access: You can revoke access permissions anytime

⚠️ Important: Backup Your Wallet

Since only you can decrypt your entries, it's crucial to backup your wallet recovery phrase. If you lose access to your wallet, we cannot help you recover your encrypted journal entries.

What We Never Share

🚫 Zero Data Selling

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. This is a core principle we will never compromise on.

❌ We Never Share

  • Private Journal Entries: Your private entries stay private, period
  • Personal Information: Any personal details you provide
  • Wallet Activity: Your transaction history outside of DiaryVault
  • Usage Patterns: How you use the platform
  • AI Interactions: Your conversations with our AI features

🤝 Limited Sharing (With Your Consent)

  • Public Entries: Only entries you explicitly mark as public
  • Marketplace Items: Journal entries you choose to sell or share
  • Tips & Rewards: Public blockchain transactions (these are inherently public)
  • Shared Collections: Content you deliberately share with others

🏛️ Legal Requirements

  • Court Orders: We may be required to provide data if legally compelled
  • Safety Issues: In cases of imminent harm or illegal activity
  • Platform Abuse: To prevent fraud or abuse of our services

📝 Note About Public Blockchain

Remember that blockchain transactions (rewards, tips, NFT mints) are inherently public and permanent. This is a feature of blockchain technology, not a privacy limitation on our part.

AI & Privacy

We use AI to enhance your journaling experience while maintaining strict privacy standards:

🤖 AI Features

  • Mood Analysis: AI analyzes the emotional tone of your entries
  • Writing Prompts: Personalized suggestions based on your writing style
  • Insights & Summaries: AI-generated reflections on your journal patterns
  • Visual Creation: Turn your entries into artistic representations

🔒 Privacy-Preserving AI

  • Local Processing: When possible, AI runs on your device
  • Encrypted Processing: Server-side AI only sees encrypted data
  • No Training Data: Your entries are never used to train AI models
  • Temporary Processing: AI analysis is done in memory and immediately discarded

🎛️ Your Control

  • Opt-In Only: All AI features are optional
  • Granular Control: Choose which entries to analyze
  • Data Deletion: AI insights can be deleted anytime
  • Export Options: Take your AI-generated content with you

🧠 Edge Computing

Where possible, we use edge computing and local AI models to analyze your content without sending it to external servers. Your thoughts stay on your device.

Minimal Tracking

🍪 Cookie-Light Approach

We use the absolute minimum tracking necessary to make the platform work. No advertising cookies, no behavioral tracking, no data brokers.

✅ Essential Cookies Only

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme and language settings
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and abuse

📊 Privacy-Focused Analytics

  • Anonymized Data: We track feature usage, not individuals
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: We don't track you across other websites
  • No Advertising IDs: We don't create advertising profiles
  • Local Storage: Preferences stored in your browser, not our servers

🚫 What We Don't Track

  • Personal Identity: No attempts to identify you personally
  • Social Media Integration: No Facebook pixels or similar tracking
  • Email Tracking: No email open tracking or click tracking
  • Location Data: We don't collect or store location information

🔍 Transparency Report

We publish an annual transparency report detailing any government requests for data, our response, and aggregate platform usage statistics.
